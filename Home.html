faq

Will my save file be fine if I update to the latest version?

Updating the game shouldn't remove the save.
Changing to beta versions also should keep the save.

However, it may not be the case for some phones so take that into consideration.

Which version should I download?

Try the normal version, if it crashes, try the shaderless version.

The game crashes for both normal and shaderless, why does that happen?

Your device may be low in memory or  unsupported. Make sure your phone has at least 2GB of RAM and/or newer CPU and GPU.

Are controllers/Gamekeyboard+ supported?

Yes. If you need to adjust the controls, go to Config and change it.
(You'll need to reach at the dark world portion of the game to find it!)

If you play with a controller it's recommended to hide the touch controls, press the 4 dots and on "Enable Touch Controls?" press "No".
If you use Gamekeyboard+ or similar apps it's recommended to rebind the ZXC keys to 123, press the 4 dots and on "Rebind Keys?" press "Yes" then follow the instructions.

Can I edit my touch controls cause it's not comfortable for me to play/messing with Hp bar

Yes, you can always edit them in the settings (Dark World settings), just go to "Settings" and select "Virtual Joystick", you'll be able to customize them.
You can also customize them on the first startup.

I want to enable borders!

You can change them in the Dark World settings.

Can I import or export my save?

Yes, you can access them at Android/data/com.mtc.deltarune/files (Using any file manager)
Be aware that if the folder doesn't exist the saves won't be accessible (you might be warned in-game), the solution is creating the folder manually on Internal or External Memory (if the folder is already there then it's an Android bug and there is nothing we can do about it.)
